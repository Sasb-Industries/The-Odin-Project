Welcome to the Roast Beast recipe page!

Roast Beef is a delicious and nutritios entree to consume and fills the heart with joy.

It is kind of a pain to make but all the ladies will be impressed that you have the ability to actually cook food.

Ingredients:

  • Meat for Roast Beast
  • Oven
  • Patience

Directions

  1. Put beast in oven at 350 degrees.
  2. Wait patiently for 45 minutes.
  3. Take out and let cool.
  4. Take out and be cool.
